I am a PhD student in the joint program between the Department of Statistics and Data Science and the Heinz College at Carnegie Mellon University, advised by Alexandra Chouldechova and Zachary Lipton.

My research interests sit at the intersection of causal inference, fairness/disparities problems, and text analysis.

Before coming to Pittsburgh, I graduated with a Bachelor of Arts in Mathematics from New York University in 2021.
